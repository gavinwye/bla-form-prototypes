Before you start

You may not be able to use this service

Based on your answers, you may not be able to complete this application online.

This could be because:

  • The vehicle is not registered in Barbados
  • You don't have the vehicle's logbook available
  • You are not the registered owner and don't have written authorisation

What you can do

  • Contact the Licensing Authority at 246-535-0085
  • Visit the office at Wildey, St. Michael to apply in person
  • Obtain written authorisation from the registered owner if needed
  • Return to this service once you have your vehicle logbook
