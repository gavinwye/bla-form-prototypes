Apply for a motor vehicle inspection

Licensing Authority, Pine, St. Michael, Barbados

Last updated: 24 March 2026  ·  Estimated time: 10 minutes (after checking eligibility)

Use this service to apply for an inspection of a motor vehicle in Barbados. Before you start, we'll check you're eligible and help you gather everything you need.

  • ✓ Quick eligibility check first
  • ✓ Document checklist to prepare you
  • ✓ 9 steps to complete your application

What you will need

  • Vehicle logbook
  • National Registration card
  • Vehicle registration certificate (if previously registered)
  • Valid insurance certificate
  • Preferred date and time for your inspection

Before you start

  • The vehicle must be registered or being registered in Barbados
  • You must be the registered owner or have written authorisation
  • The vehicle must be physically present in Barbados for inspection
