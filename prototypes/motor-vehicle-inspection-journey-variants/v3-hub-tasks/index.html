Apply for a motor vehicle inspection

Licensing Authority, Pine, St. Michael, Barbados

Use this service to apply for a motor vehicle inspection. Complete three tasks to submit your application. You can complete them in any order and return to any task at any time.

This application has 3 tasks:

1

Tell us about yourself

Your name, contact details and address

2

Tell us about your vehicle

Vehicle details, registration and technical specifications

3

Book your inspection

Choose a date, time and describe the vehicle's purpose

How to apply

Select "Start application" below. You will be taken to a task list where you can complete each task in any order. When all three tasks are done, you can review and submit your application.

Estimated time: 10–12 minutes

Start application

What you will need

  • Vehicle logbook (log book)
  • National Registration card or passport
  • Vehicle registration certificate (if previously registered)
  • Your preferred inspection date and time

Before you start

You can save your progress and return to complete unfinished tasks at any time during the same browser session.

If you need help, contact the Licensing Authority on 246-535-0500 or visit the office at Pine, St. Michael.
