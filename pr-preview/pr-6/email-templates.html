Email templates

Confirmation emails for applicants and notification emails for the BLA, for each of the 22 online forms.

Each form has two email templates:

  • Applicant email – sent to the person who submitted the form, confirming receipt and explaining what happens next
  • BLA email – sent to the Barbados Licensing Authority (enquiries@bla.gov.bb), notifying staff of a new submission with applicant details and a summary
