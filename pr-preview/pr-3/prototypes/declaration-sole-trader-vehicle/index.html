Declaration Form – Sole Trader Vehicle Registration for Private Use

Barbados Licensing Authority

Section 2, Road Traffic (Amendment) Act, 2018-31

Use this service to submit the statutory declaration required when you register a vehicle for private use as a sole trader.

Warning

Any person who knowingly makes a false statement in this declaration is liable to a fine of BDS$5,000 or 12 months imprisonment.

How to apply

You can complete this declaration online using the form below.

What you will need to share

Before you start, make sure you have the following information available:

  • Your personal details
  • Vehicle chassis and engine details
